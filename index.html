verdure

[vur-jer]

 

greenness; freshness in general; flourishing condition; vigor

 

 

VERDURE is an indoors-plant shop situated in Casili, Consolacion, Cebu City. Our shop sells all kinds of indoor plants such as succulents plants, hanging plants, foliage plants, etc.

 

VERDURE started as a small business in 2015 by Wynnona Rodriguez by selling succulent plants in 2015 to relatives, friends, and acquaintances. At that time there was no name, physical store or branding yet. Overtime, the business expanded and gradually developed to what it is today.

 

Our mission is to have people bring in natural freshness in their homes, offices, bedrooms, etc. with plants that we have cared and grown ourselves.

 

Come visit us or browse through our website. Enjoy!
